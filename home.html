About Me

Hi! I'm Odette Yang, a senior at Marriotts Ridge High School. I am a student athlete and musician, and I love volunteering for community initiatives! I was born in Montreal, Canada, and my family immigrated to the US when I was 3. From an early age, I found a passion for music when my mom signed me up for piano lessons at the local studio. I also LOVE reading and cooking! In my free time, I also love traveling. I go north to snowboard in Vermont in the winter months and to the beach in the summer. When I'm not coding, I'm probaby cooking, reading, playing music, or going on a run. Thanks for visiting my website, I hope you enjoy!
